Who We Are

Built on Integrity, Crafted with Pride

Brodin Construction has been serving Colorado homeowners for over 15 years. We started as a small family operation with one simple promise: do the job right, treat people fairly, and stand behind every nail we drive.

Today we're a full-service general contractor tackling everything from kitchen remodels and bathroom upgrades to decks, fences, concrete, and roofing — but that original promise hasn't changed one bit.

  • Transparent pricing — no surprise add-ons
  • All work guaranteed with written warranty
  • Locally owned and community-focused
What We Do

Our Services

We handle a wide range of residential projects — no job too big or too small. If it takes skill and integrity to get right, we're the crew for it.

Remodels & Renovations

Kitchens, bathrooms, basements, additions — we transform your existing space into exactly what you need. Quality finishes, clean work, done on schedule.

Concrete Work

Driveways, patios, walkways, foundations, and retaining walls. Precision poured and properly finished to handle Colorado's tough freeze-thaw cycles year after year.

Decks & Fences
